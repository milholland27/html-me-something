    Penguin & Puffin Coast

    The Penguin & Puffin Coast at the St. Louis Zoo is a Milholland family favorite.

    The habitat opened in 2003 and was the first walk-through sub-Antarctic penguin exhibit in North America.

    Grizzly Ridge

    Grizzly Ridge at the Zoo opened this year and is amazing.

    You can get up-close and personal with the Grizzly Bears.

    Herpetarium

    PYTHON! No, not the Python coding language.

    This is a one of the many reptiles found in the Herpetarium.

    This building is one of the original structures at the St. Louis Zoo, dating back to the early 1900's.

    Fragile Forest

    The St. Louis Zoo Fragile Forest is an outdoor habitat for orangutans, chimpanzees and gorillas.

    The St. Louis Zoo supports programs all over the world to help endanged animals in their natural habitats.
